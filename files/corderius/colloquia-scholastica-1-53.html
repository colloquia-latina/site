Persōnae:
Galatīnus, Buchardus
Galatīnus Eugē, dīmissī sumus ad lūdum, audīstīne?
Buchardus Quidnī audīrem, cum egomet adsuērim?
Galatīnus Placetne paulisper cōnfābulārī? deinde lūdēmus ūnā.
Buchardus Māllem ego prius lūdere.
Galatīnus Atquī difficile est lūdum abrumpere.
Buchardus Plānē vērum dīcis, et ego in mē sīc experior.
Galatīnus Quoniam igitur placet tibi mea ratiō, dā nōbīs aliquid argūmentī ad cōnfābulandum.
Buchardus Īmō tuum est dare, ut quī mē lacessīverīs.
Galatīnus Aequum dīcis; redde nōmina Latīnē, quae tibi Anglicē prōpōnam.
Buchardus Quā dē rē prōpōnēs?
Galatīnus Dē supellectilī.
Buchardus Tentābō respondēre, modo nē plūra quam decem prōpōnās.
Galatīnus Ēnumerābō in digitīs, nē forte numerum excēdam. Audī igitur.
Buchardus Isthic sum.
Galatīnus A cupboard.
Buchardus Abacus.
Galatīnus A bench.
Buchardus Sella.
Galatīnus A candlestick.
Buchardus Candēlābrum.
Galatīnus A caldron.
Buchardus Ahēnum.
Galatīnus Ā pair of bellows.
Buchardus Follis.
Galatīnus A pillow.
Buchardus Pulvīnar.
Galatīnus A balster.
Buchardus Cervīcal.
Galatīnus Linen-cloth.
Buchardus Linteum.
Galatīnus A pot of earth.
Buchardus Ōlla.
Galatīnus A flagon of wine.
Buchardus Oenophorum.
Galatīnus Errāstī semel.
Buchardus Ubī?
Galatīnus Dīxistī ‘linteum’ prō ‘lōdīce’.
Buchardus Fateor: dēbeō tibi semel victōriam.
Galatīnus Nunc vicissim prōpōne, ut redimās, sī potes.
Buchardus Vīs respondēre dē edūliīs?
Galatīnus Ut libet.
Buchardus Fresh meat.
Galatīnus Carō recēns.
Buchardus Pork.
Galatīnus Suilla.
Buchardus Venison.
Galatīnus Ferīna.
Buchardus Venison of a wild boar.
Galatīnus Aprūgna.
Buchardus Sod milk.
Galatīnus Lac dēcoctum.
Buchardus Whey.
Galatīnus Sērum, vel sērum lactis.
Buchardus New cheese.
Galatīnus Cāseus recēns.
Buchardus Pottage.
Galatīnus Iūs carnium.
Buchardus Fish sod.
Galatīnus Piscis clīxus.
Buchardus Pickle.
Galatīnus Condīmentum.
Buchardus Falleris.
Galatīnus Quid ergō est?
Buchardus Intīnctus.
Galatīnus Condīmentum volō.
Buchardus At ego nōlō contendere.
Galatīnus Quis contendit? cōnsulāmus.
Buchardus Quīn prius lūdāmus; illud fīet posterius.
Galatīnus Age, fīet, nē āmittāmus lūdendī occāsiōnem.
