Persōnae:
Magister, Theophilus
Magister
Hodiē igitur Petrum convēnistī?
Theophilus
Hodiē.
Magister
Ubī?
Theophilus
In templō.
Magister
Quotā hōrā?
Theophilus
Octāvā mātūtīnā.
Magister
Nunquid rogāstī quandō sit reditūrus scholam?
Theophilus
Rogāvī.
Magister
Quid ille?
Theophilus
Nesciō, inquit.
Magister
Dēbuistī illum ad reditum exhortārī.
Theophilus
Id ego fēcī, et multīs quidem verbīs.
Magister
Bene fēcistī, sed quid ille respondit?
Theophilus
Sē adhūc ā patre dētinērī ad frūctūs colligendōs.
Magister
Quid sī ad ipsum patrem scrībās dē statū nostrō scholasticō? Fortasse enim movēbitur, ut fīlium citius remittat.
Theophilus
Sī tibi ita vidētur, faciam, idque dīligenter.
Magister
Fac igitur prīmō quōque tempore; sed audī, scrībe plānissimē, deinde līterās tuās mihi ostende priusquam dēs perferendās.
Theophilus
Sēdulō faciam, praeceptor.
