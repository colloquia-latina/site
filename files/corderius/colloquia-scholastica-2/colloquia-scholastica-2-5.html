Persōnae:
Observātor, Puerī garrientēs
Puerī garrientēs
Quid vōs hīc agitis, puerī? Mihi vidēminī garrīre, et nūgās agere.
Puerī garrientēs
[Quīdam puer] Longē falleris: nam repetimus ūnā.
Observātor
Quā dē rē?
Puerī garrientēs
Dē verbīs anōmalīs, id quod reddendum est hōrā tertiā.
Observātor
Bene facitis.
Puerī garrientēs
Vīs audīre nostrum colloquium?
Observātor
Īmō pergite; maius opus moveō; volō tendere laqueōs Pīcīs et Grāculīs.
Puerī garrientēs
In āreā multōs ad sōlem inveniēs.
Observātor
‘Rētibus est illīc praeda parāta meīs.’
